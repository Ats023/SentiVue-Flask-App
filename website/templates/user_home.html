Welcome to SentiVue!

SentiVue is a robust sentiment analysis tool that assists users in analyzing and summarizing product(s) reviews with its intuitive charts and graphs.

Why SentiVue?

In today's fast-paced digital landscape, customer feedback is a goldmine of insights, but sifting through countless reviews can be overwhelming. Sentivue empowers you to effortlessly navigate through numerous customer sentiments, ensuring you make data-driven decisions with confidence.

Utilizing machine learning pedadogies, it categorizes reviews into 'positive', 'negative', and 'neutral'. It also provides users with polarity and subjectivity data to have a further overview of their dataset.
